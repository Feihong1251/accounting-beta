UX/UI Designer
1 position
Full-time
CW Tower, Ratchadaphisek, Bangkok / Onsite
Role Overview:
As our UX/UI Designer, you’ll work closely with product managers, developers, and stakeholders to craft engaging user experiences from concept to execution. You’ll conduct user research, create wireframes, design high-fidelity mockups, and collaborate in bringing beautiful, functional interfaces to life.

Responsibilities:
  • Conduct user research and translate insights into user journeys, wireframes, and prototypes
  • Design visually appealing, user-centered interfaces for web and/or mobile platforms
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define and implement innovative solutions
  • Use data and feedback to iterate and refine designs
  • Maintain and contribute to a cohesive design system
  • Stay up to date on industry trends and best practices in UX/UI and digital product design

    • Requirements:
  • 2+ years of experience in UX/UI design (or a strong portfolio demonstrating relevant skills)
  • Proficiency in tools like Figma, Adobe XD, Sketch, or similar
  • Strong understanding of design principles, typography, and color theory
  • Experience conducting user testing and applying insights to improve UX
  • Ability to think both strategically and tactically
  • Great communication skills and a collaborative mindset

    • Nice to Have:
  • Experience designing for SaaS or e-commerce
  • Familiarity with HTML/CSS or front-end development basics
  • Animation/motion design skills

    • Why Join Us?:
  • Competitive salary and benefits
  • Flexible working hours and remote options
  • A creative, inclusive team that values your ideas
  • Opportunities to grow and shape the future of our design team

    CW Tower, Ratchadaphisek, Bangkok / Onsite
    Role Overview:
    We are seeking a vigilant and proactive Security Operator to join our Security Operations team. In this role, you will monitor and respond to security alerts, help protect our systems and data, and support incident response efforts. You’ll play a key part in ensuring the safety and integrity of our digital infrastructure.

    Responsibilities:
  • Monitor security systems, logs, and alerts (SIEM, IDS/IPS, endpoint protection)

  • Respond to security events and assist in containment, eradication, and recovery

  • Generate reports on incidents, vulnerabilities, and system status
  • Support vulnerability assessments and compliance checks
  • Collaborate with IT and security teams to enhance system protections

  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced, 24/7 environment (including shifts or on-call if required)
